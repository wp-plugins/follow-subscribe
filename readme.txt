== Description ==

**Try the plugin at: http://sd.linksalpha.com**

**Enables Sharing of your website to 10+ Social Networks, including:**

1. Google +1
1. Facebook Like
1. Twitter button
1. LinkedIn 
1. Pinterest
1. StumbleUpon
1. YouTube
1. About.me
1. Xing
1. foursquare
1. RSS

**List of Features**

* Social Sharing Buttons/Icons in the sidebar.
* Displays Social Buttons with count of activity for Facebook, and Google Plus.
* Displays follow button for Twitter.
* Displays Social Icons for LinkedIn, Pinterest, StumbleUpon, YouTube, About.me, Xing, foursquare, and RSS Feed.
* Offers consistent UI: aligned buttons and uniform color selection.

== Installation ==

1. Upload Follow-subscribe.zip to '/wp-content/plugins/' directory and unzip it.
2. Activate the Plugin from "Manage Plugins" window
